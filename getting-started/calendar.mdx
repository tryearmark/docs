## What it does

Connect your Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook to Earmark so your scheduled meetings appear automatically in the sidebar. Your day at a glance. Instantly see what's coming up, how many meetings you have left, and how long you have. Join and start capturing with one click.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Settings">
Go to **Settings > Calendar** in the Earmark desktop app.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose your provider">
Connect either **Google Calendar** or **Microsoft Outlook**. You can have one provider connected at a time.
</Step>
<Step title="Authorize access">
Complete the OAuth flow in your browser to grant Earmark read access to your calendar events.
</Step>
<Step title="Select visible calendars">
After connecting, choose which calendars appear in Earmark. Toggle individual calendars on or off (for example, show your team calendar but hide personal events).
</Step>
</Steps>

## Desktop notifications

Enable **One click join and capture** in calendar settings to receive a notification before each meeting. The notification includes a button to join the meeting and start recording instantly.

## Menu bar countdown (macOS)

On macOS, Earmark can display your next meeting and a live countdown directly in the system menu bar. Toggle this in **Settings > Calendar > Menu bar**.

## Switching providers

To switch from Google to Outlook (or vice versa), disconnect your current calendar first, then connect the new one. Earmark will prompt you to clear any stale upcoming meetings from the previous provider to keep the sidebar clean.
